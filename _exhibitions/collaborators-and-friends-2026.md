Presented by the Ilan Lael Foundation, this one-day exhibit brings together a never-before-seen piece by the late James Hubbell alongside works by longtime collaborators Peter Mitten and Wendell Perry. The exhibition features sculptures, watercolors, and collages spanning over four decades of partnership, including the public sculpture *Sea Nymph* on Shelter Island.

Free admission; all proceeds benefit the Ilan Lael Foundation.
